satvp

IPW User Command
Category - Environmental Physics and Properties: Point

NAME

satvp - saturation vapor pressure from dew point temperature

SYNOPSIS

satvp [ -K ] [ -m ]

DESCRIPTION

satvp is one of a group of commands designed to simulate energy, climate, or hydrologic processes or properties at a point. Most were originally written as "qdips" (Quick and Dirty Image Processing System) programs by D. Marks, during the 1980's at UCSB.

satvp converts dew point temp to sat. vapor pressure Reads dew point temp from standard input; Dew point temp. input in C (default) or K; Calculates vapor pressure in Pascals (default) or mb; Output is to standard output;

(see General Meterology, Byers, 1974)

OPTIONS

-K
The input Dew point temperatures are in degrees K (default: C).

-m
The output values for vapor pressure are in millibars. (default: Pa).

OPERANDS

none

EXAMPLES

To estimate saturation vapor pressure from air temperature: 

	satvp < ta_vector
